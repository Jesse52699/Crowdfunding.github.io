Welcome to Life Aromatherapy - an exploration of the senses...
Founded and formulated in 2005 by Aromatherapist Carrie A Keller. The Life Aromatherapy product line is 100% pure, all-natural, highly concentrated, effective and carries a vibration and quality that is unmistakable.

My first experience with the use of plants began at age 8 when I would pick magnolia flowers to make my own "perfume" by pressing the petals in water and letting the infusion sit for weeks. Little did I know that years later I would formulate my own line of luxury perfumes and all-natural, essential oil based products.


Life Chakra Sprays™

The formulation process is what I enjoy the most (aside from working with people). It's such a creative, meditative process. I feel these products are part of me. My hope is that people feel the intention and integrity with my work.


Life Essentials Skin Care Line™

My global vision in creating the line was to help humanity. Life Aromatherapy products were formulated to bring a little light and support into the lives of the people they touch. I wanted to weave my spiritual work into my products to create a deeper meaning other than just having a nice smelling, effective products. I wanted people to feel like they were buying something special. Something that would help them in their lives. My hope is that I've done that through my line and that I bring inspiration and joy to all those that I encounter.

Risks and challenges

We've spent over $20,000 of our own money in product development and marketing to get Life Aromatherapy to this point and are excited about showcasing the line at COEUR NYC and Elements Showcase NYC.

As a new business, there are a number of costs that go into manufacturing and marketing the product and trade show participation. COEUR will be our first trade show adn we will need to order materials to make the product and be prepared to fulfill orders. The cost of the Elements Showcase is equally expensive but could truly elevate our business. We feel participation in these shows is essential for growth and exposure.

If we hit our fundraising goal, your money will go directly to the trade show fees and production and would allow Life Aromatherapy to move forward in making great products for our customers.
